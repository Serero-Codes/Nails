About

More about Us

Sienah Sparkle


Hello, I'm Sienah Sparkle, a young entrepreneur from Makhubidung, a small village in Tzaneen. I’m currently based in Pretoria, where I’ve turned my passion for nails into a full-fledged business. Nails are not just a hobby for me—they are my passion, my inspiration, and the way I express myself creatively. I’ve always found joy in making my customers feel beautiful, and that’s what keeps me going every day.

The Journey


I’ve been doing nails for the past five years, and it’s been quite a journey of learning, growing, and improving. I started out by teaching myself, watching endless tutorials, and experimenting with different designs. Of course, there were mistakes along the way—but each one taught me something new. Over time, I went from a complete beginner to a self-taught professional, and I’m proud of how far I’ve come. Moving from my small village to Pretoria was a huge step. The city was a whole new world, and I had to work hard to establish my business. But I was determined. My work speaks for itself, and as I continued to hone my craft, my clientele grew. Seeing the smiles on my customers’ faces after a session is one of the most satisfying parts of my job.
